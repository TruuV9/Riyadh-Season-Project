Roshn Front

As a part of its efforts to bring a new way of integrated living to the Kingdom, ROSHN has expanded its footprint in Riyadh by acquiring the ROSHN Front shopping and business area, located adjacent to ROSHN’s flagship development, SEDRA. The acquisition enables ROSHN to become a multi-asset class real estate developer and manager of global significance. An extraordinary urban experience that reimagines how we work, play, dine, shop, and share space in the north of Riyadh. Integrating human-centered design and social spaces, ROSHN Front isn't just a place - it's a living hub of connection, curiosity and innovation.

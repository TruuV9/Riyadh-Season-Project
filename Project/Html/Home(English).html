About Riyadh

Season

Riyadh Season is one of the world’s largest winter

entertainment event. Variety of food from around

the world, courtesy of exclusive restaurants plus

countless exciting surprises. The festivities capture

the essence of Saudi, from its Najdi heritage to its

present day dynamism.
